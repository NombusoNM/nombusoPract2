Biography and Background

Mbalenhle Mdletshe was born in Johannesburg, and she went to school in Germiston. She studies BEng and majors in Computer Engineering.

Biography and Background

In her spare time, Mbalenhle enjoys knitting and sewing, finding joy in creating unique designs with every stitch. These crafts provide her with a sense of relaxation and accomplishment as she brings her ideas to life. She takes pride in the patience and precision required, always eager to learn new techniques. With a passion for handmade artistry, Mbalenhle embraces the timeless charm of working with yarn and fabric.
