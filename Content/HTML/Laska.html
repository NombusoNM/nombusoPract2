Biography and Background

Lola Mdletshe was born in Durban, and she went to school in Vryheid. She studies BSc degree and majors in Biochemistry and Microbiolgy.

Hobbies

In her spare time, Lola enjoys 3D modeling and sewing, finding joy in creating unique designs with every click. These crafts provide her with a sense of relaxation and accomplishment as she brings her ideas to life. She takes pride in the patience and precision required, always eager to learn new techniques. With a passion for handmade and digital artistry, Lola embraces the timeless charm of working with fabric and computers.
