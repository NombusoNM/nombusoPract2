Biography and Background

Laska Mdletshe was born in Durban, and she went to school in Vryheid. She studies BSc and majors in Architecture.

Biography and Background

In her spare time, Laksa enjoys knitting and sewing, finding joy in creating unique designs with every stitch. These crafts provide her with a sense of relaxation and accomplishment as she brings her ideas to life. She takes pride in the patience and precision required, always eager to learn new techniques. With a passion for handmade artistry, LASKA embraces the timeless charm of working with yarn and fabric.
