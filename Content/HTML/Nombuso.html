Biography and Background

Nombuso Mdletshe was born in Margate, and she went to school in Pinetown. She studies BSc and majors in Architecture.

Hobbies

In her spare time, Nombuso enjoys knitting and sewing, finding joy in creating unique designs with every stitch. These crafts provide her with a sense of relaxation and accomplishment as she brings her ideas to life. She takes pride in the patience and precision required, always eager to learn new techniques. With a passion for handmade artistry, Laska embraces the timeless charm of working with yarn and fabric.
