Biography and Background

Nothando Mdletshe was born in Western Cape, and she went to school in Paarl. She studies BIT Information Systems and majors in Data Science.

Biography and Background

Nothando finds joy in tufting, where she makes intricate rugs and coasters. Beyond the tufting, she expresses her athletism through running. The meditative rhythm of her gait provides a sense of relaxation, fulfillment and regulation.
